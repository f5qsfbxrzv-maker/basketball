## Success Metrics
- **Accuracy**: >55% on spreads, >60% on moneylines
- **ROI**: Target 15-25% annual return
- **Sharpe Ratio**: >1.5
- **Maximum Drawdown**: <20%
- **Bet Volume**: 200-500 bets per season

## Risk Management Principles
1. Never risk more than 2% of bankroll on single bet
2. Maximum 10% of bankroll at risk at any time
3. Stop trading if drawdown exceeds 20%
4. Regular model retraining and validation
5. Strict separation of paper and live trading
